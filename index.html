How it works: This tool processes RTF content directly in your browser. No data is sent to any server - your content stays private!

Supported RTF features:

  • Basic text formatting (bold, italic, underline)
  • Font and size information (ignored in plain text output)
  • Paragraph breaks and line breaks
  • Special characters and Unicode
